Dubai’s Trusted Transport & Industrial Partner

Since 2000, BASMA Al Madina Transport LLC has been serving Dubai with reliable transport and industrial solutions. From a small start, we grew into a leading name trusted by government projects, top developers, and multinational companies across the UAE.

Based in Al Quoz Industrial Area, we provide specialized transport, drainage solutions, water supply systems, and premium diesel trading. With modern equipment, skilled professionals, and 24/7 emergency response, we continue to deliver quality services that clients can rely on.
